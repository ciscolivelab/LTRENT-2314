    Lab Introduction

    Company ACME is a multi-international company where it has branches world wide which include two Data Centers in San Jose, RTP and few regional hubs in different continents as well as remote branches world wide.

    Lab Design

    This lab is designed to help you understanding various advanced Viptela SDWAN features based on a large multi-international company's design requirements. The lab is built with a NFV software to simulate ACME's topology running in dCloud. The basic connectivity, SDWAN feature templates, devices templates and some lists for policy are pre-configured for you.

    As shown in the above diagram:

    • Both DC1 and DC2 have two cEdges which connect to three transports
    • RH(reginal hub) has one cEdge which connects to three transports
    • Both DC1-WEs and DC2-WEs are running eBGP with DC routers, BGP is pre-configured on the DC routers.
    • Remote1 has one cEdge which connects to private1 and LTE transports
    • Remote2 has one cEdge which connects to private2 and LTE transports
